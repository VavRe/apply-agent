Faculty
=======

Our faculty are established leaders in the field of intelligent systems. Their research spans the disciplines of machine learning, computer vision, robotic motion control, micro- and nano-robotics, and haptics.

#### imprs-is

The International Max Planck Research School for Intelligent Systems is an interdisciplinary doctoral program offered by the [Max Planck Institute for Intelligent for Intelligent Systems](https://is.mpg.de/en), the [University of Stuttgart](https://www.uni-stuttgart.de/en/), and the [University of Tübingen](https://uni-tuebingen.de/en).

Created in 2017, the program is now the largest doctoral program of its kind in the Max Planck Society and is guaranteed funding through at least 2030.
